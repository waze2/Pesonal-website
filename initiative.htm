What SP Has Done In Campus

The First Initiative

SP has installed more than a hundred water dispensers around the campus. This initiative started by SP is very effective as students are able to bring their own bottles to school instead of buying plastic bottles. It also further benefits students by helping to reduce the cost of having to purchase their own water.

The Second Initiative

By the end of 2023, SP should have completed the installation of the photovoltaic panels across the campus. These new installations provide us with renewable sources of energy, which reduces the reliance on burning fossil fuels to power up the campus. Moreover, this initiative reduces SP’s carbon footprint and helps our nation reach sustainability goals.

The Third Initiative

SP has planted more than 200 species of flora around the campus (230 species to be exact). This initiative not only makes the campus more vibrant and picturesque, but it greatly aids in reducing carbon emissions in the air.

The Fourth Initiative

SP has made it compulsory for all students to take 5 sustainability modules in their diploma courses. Through the 5 modules, it prepares students for the future of work in a green economy. Empowering our people with the relevant knowledge and skills about sustainability is a recipe for success in protecting our environment.

The Fifth Initiative

SP has a student based “environment club” which advocates driving environmental consciousness among their peers and the community through a variety of campaigns and social media content. Students who are interested in providing a service for the environment can join the club to spread their positive message. Ultimately, it can create a culture for saving the environment within SP’s student body.

Why Save The Earth?

We should save the mother earth so that our future generations can live in a safe environment. We can save the earth by saving trees, natural vegetation, water, natural resources, electricity, etc. We should strictly follow all the possible measures to control the environmental pollution and global warming.
