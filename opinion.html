How Can We Save The Earth?

What's Happening To Our Planet

Since the start of industrialization in the 19th century, we intensified the use of fossil fuels releasing large amount of carbon dioxide into our environment. This causes the phenonmenon named the greenhouse effect which is the main driver for climate change.

Effects of climate change

Climate change has made extreme weather events such as floods, droughts, and forest fires more likely and severe. This can take many lives, and destroy livelihoods. Furthermore, rising temperatures is another effect of climate change. This has caused the melting of polar icecaps, destroying habitats and cause sea levels to rise.

My Opinion

Personally, I feel that everyone should adopt the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse and Recycle) in order to save the Earth. Where reduce is the most important R followed by reuse and recycle.

REDUCE

Reduce is the consumption of less resources. A way for us to carry out Reduce would be turning off the light switches when not in use, or ordering less food if unable to consume the whole portion. What is most important is how mindful we are when it comes to ways to reduce wastage.

REUSE

Reuse is giving an object about to be thrown a new life. For example, old clothes can be repurposed into rags used for cleaning. Another instance would be purchasing reusable items like tote bags, handkerchiefs, and water bottles. Overall, Reuse helps us to prevent wastage via giving a new purpose to objects or using multi-use items.

RECYCLE

Recycle is converting waste into usable products. What we can do as an individual is sort out our waste according to its material and then dispose of it into recycling bins. For example, plastic, paper and metal. The waste is then filtered and cleaned, ready to be used for materials for production of items.

My Personal Goals For The Environment

Goal 1

Reduce the amount of waste I put out. Personally, I feel that I am a very wasteful person and I wish to not damage the environment. Hence the measures I am taking will be carrying reusable bags with me wherever I go, using a handkerchief instead of using tissue paper, and I will take shorter showers.

Goal 2

Grow some plants in my house. Due to globalization, many trees have been deforested causing carbon emissions to be at an all time high. Hence, I feel I have a duty to aid in reducing carbon emissions for the sake of our future. Some plants I want to grow in my home include money plant, snake plant, and the umbrella plant. These plants are easy to grow at home and definitely help in reducing carbon emissions, even though the results are miniscule.

Goal 3

To advocate the message “Love our planet”. I plan to create blog pages on various social media platforms like TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook to spread my message. Examples of content I will put up on the social media platforms include DIY videos teaching others how to repurpose objects, and vlogs of me growing my plants at home!
